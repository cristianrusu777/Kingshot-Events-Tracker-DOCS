# Reviewing applications

## Review procedure

1. Confirm the correct kingdom and cycle. Ministers cannot review another kingdom’s candidates.
2. Filter the list by status, stage, eligibility or availability, then open the candidate detail.
3. Verify the player link, required identifier where configured, alliance/context, stage choices, resources, True Gold, custom answers and contact preference.
4. Resolve missing identity information by linking or creating the appropriate player record where your access permits. Use **Needs review** when the decision or information needs follow-up.
5. Accept a candidate for consideration, reject it, or keep it on standby. Acceptance is not a reservation.
6. Read configured eligibility and ranking information as decision support, then use the [planner](schedule-planner.md).

## What the indicators mean

Eligibility can include required identity, membership, required fields and configured resource/True Gold thresholds. A stage can be configured so all rules are required or so any configured rule is enough. Ranking/score helps compare candidates after those choices; it does not override explicit unavailable times, capacity, overlap checks or administrator decisions.

::: tip
For a contested slot, review the time match and unplaced reason alongside score. The normal automatic strategy prioritizes exact time match before score; see the full [selection algorithm](automatic-placement.md).
:::

## Escalation

Ask the King or Supreme Admin to resolve wrong kingdom scope, missing platform-level settings, sender configuration or access-denied problems. Do not use a resource value or a comment as proof of account identity.
