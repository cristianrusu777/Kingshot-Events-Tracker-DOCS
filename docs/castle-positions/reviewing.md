# Reviewing applications

Kings and Ministers open **Castle Positions**, select the cycle, and use the applications list. Ministers are locked to their assigned Kingdom; Supreme Admins can select a Kingdom.

1. Filter by status, resource, eligibility, or availability.
2. Open a candidate to check player link, alliance, stage choices, resources, True Gold, custom answers, review notes, and email preference.
3. Resolve a missing player link or identifier before scheduling. Use **Needs review** for incomplete information.
4. Read eligibility and ranking as decision support. Rules can require identity, membership, required fields, minimum resources, or minimum True Gold; a stage can require all rules or any configured rule.
5. Accept, reject, or keep a clear follow-up note.
6. Place accepted or linked candidates in the planner.

Scores, ranks, and suggestions assist comparison; they do not replace the Kingdom team's judgement. An accepted candidate can remain unplaced when capacity or availability is insufficient.
