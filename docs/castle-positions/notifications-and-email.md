# Notifications and email

Castle Positions uses Kingdom-specific email settings. Administrators can enable or disable the sender and choose **automatic**, **manual**, or **disabled** notification mode.

When a finalized stage is published in automatic mode, affected applicants with valid opted-in contact email are sent their result. The notification log records sent, failed, skipped, and disabled outcomes. Failed delivery can be retried where the notification controls are available.

No contact email is required when Castle Positions email is unavailable. Email is not a replacement for checking **My Castle Positions**: an opted-out contact, missing email, disabled sender, spam filter, or delivery failure can prevent receipt.
