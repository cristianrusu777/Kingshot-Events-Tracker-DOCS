# Application statuses

| Status | Meaning | Usually set by | What to expect |
|---|---|---|---|
| Submitted / Linked | Received; Linked means the player identity was resolved | Submission or reviewer | Wait for review |
| Standby | Player identity is not resolved enough for scheduling | Submission or reviewer | Check details; the team may contact you |
| Needs review | Information, identity, eligibility, or a decision needs checking | King or Minister | Provide clarification if asked |
| Accepted | Approved for consideration | King or Minister | Acceptance is not a guaranteed slot |
| Rejected | Declined and cannot be scheduled | King or Minister | Contact the team if needed |
| Scheduled | Has an assignment | Schedule team | Check whether it is provisional or published |
| Changed | Application or appointment changed and needs attention | Schedule change | Check the current appointment |
| Cancelled / Archived | No longer active or retained as history | Lifecycle action | Contact the team only if unexpected |

Schedule cards can also be provisional, confirmed, completed, changed, or cancelled. Status changes are controlled by the workflow; they are not automatic promises of a later result.
