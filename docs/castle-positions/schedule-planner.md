# Schedule planner

The planner is a time-by-position board for one stage. Rows are UTC appointment times; columns are configured castle positions. A cell is a slot, with capacity, reservation state, conflicts, and an assigned player or placeholder.

Candidate cards can show an avatar or placeholder, alliance, availability, eligibility, priority/rank, and resources. Open a card for full application details.

1. Select a stage with a date and active position.
2. Review conflicts and unplaced candidates.
3. Drag a candidate to a compatible slot, or use assignment controls and manual player search where available.
4. Save, then review assignments and conflicts before finalizing.

Dragging cannot override capacity, status, identity, or conflict checks. An unplaced candidate may mean no compatible capacity is available. The density control helps with larger boards.
