# Premium Features

Premium plans unlock extra capabilities on top of the free tier. This page lists every [premium feature](../getting-started/glossary.md#premium-feature) in the app, grouped so you can find what matters to you. Which ones you actually get depends on your [effective plan](effective-plan.md) (or an accepted [grant](../getting-started/glossary.md#grant) that carries them).

## How a feature turns on

A premium feature becomes active when **either**:

1. your effective plan includes it, **or**
2. your alliance holds an **accepted** kingdom grant whose plan includes it (and the feature is one that can be shared through a grant).

Until then, the feature appears **locked** — usually as a tab or card labelled "(Premium)" with a prompt to request a subscription. Locked features never show your data; they just show the upgrade prompt.

## Analytics & insight features

| Feature | What it unlocks |
|---|---|
| **Advanced Analytics** | Deeper dashboards, more comparisons, and finer filters. |
| **Extra Charts** | Additional charts for events, players, and alliance trends. |
| **Stage Analytics** | Analysis of staged events, comparing performance between stages. |
| **Instance Internal Analytics** | Deeper summaries inside a single event instance, including how scores are spread. |
| **Player Analytics Across Events** | Follow one player across multiple event instances — attendance consistency and score progression over time. *(Alliance-focused.)* |
| **Custom Analytics Builder** | Build your own tables and charts by scope, event, date range, metric, and grouping. **See the note below.** |

> **A note on the Custom Analytics Builder:** the Custom tab is present in the app, but its full unlocked behavior hasn't been confirmed for this documentation. If your plan includes it, open the Custom tab to see what's available in your version rather than relying on a fixed description here. Treat it as "available, details may vary."

## Recommendation features

| Feature | What it unlocks |
|---|---|
| **Smart Recommendations** | Rule-based suggestions for cleanup, participation, and follow-up. |
| **Alliance Recommendations** | Alliance-level follow-up suggestions and watchlists. *(Alliance-focused.)* |
| **Player Recommendations** | Flags players who need attention, are reward-ready, or need manual review. *(Alliance-focused.)* |

## Reporting & capacity features

| Feature | What it unlocks |
|---|---|
| **Premium Report Exports** | Richer export options and premium report packaging. |
| **Larger Storage** | Bigger screenshot and import storage limits. This one works through your plan's limits rather than a button you press — a premium plan simply has more room. |

## Kingdom-to-alliance sharing

| Feature | What it unlocks |
|---|---|
| **Granted Kingdom Analytics** | Lets an alliance that **accepted a kingdom grant** view aggregate kingdom analytics. This only appears if the accepted grant specifically carries this feature — see the note under [Accept a Premium Offer](accept-grant.md). |

## Which plan carries which features

Broadly:

- **Premium Alliance** carries the alliance-focused features (player-across-events, alliance and player recommendations) plus the shared analytics and reporting extras.
- **Premium Kingdom** carries kingdom-level features plus **Granted Kingdom Analytics**, and lets the King share eligible features with alliances through [grants](kingdom-grants.md).

Not every feature can be passed down through a grant. When a King grants premium, the alliance receives only the **shareable** features the grant carries — see [Accept a Premium Offer](accept-grant.md).

The exact feature set on *your* plan is shown in your Subscription & Usage panel and on the [Support & Plans](request-subscription.md) page. That's the authoritative list for your platform.
