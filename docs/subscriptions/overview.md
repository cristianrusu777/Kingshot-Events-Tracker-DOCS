# Subscriptions & Usage — Overview

Every kingdom and alliance in the tracker runs on a **[plan](../getting-started/glossary.md#plan)**. Your plan decides two things:

1. **How much you can store** — limits (called [quotas](../getting-started/glossary.md#quota--limit)) on players, events, screenshots, storage, and so on.
2. **Which extra features you get** — [premium features](../getting-started/glossary.md#premium-feature) like advanced analytics and recommendations.

You don't need to buy anything to use the tracker. Everyone starts on a **free tier** that covers normal use. Premium plans simply raise the limits and switch on extra features. This page explains the moving parts; the rest of this section covers each in detail.

## The pieces, in plain terms

- **[Plan](../getting-started/glossary.md#plan)** — your tier. There are free tiers and premium tiers, for kingdoms and for alliances.
- **[Effective plan](../getting-started/glossary.md#effective-plan)** — the plan that *actually applies to you right now*. Because a plan can come from a few different places, the app works out which one wins. See [Which Plan Applies to You](effective-plan.md).
- **[Quota / limit](../getting-started/glossary.md#quota--limit)** — the ceiling on each kind of thing (players, screenshots, etc.). As you approach a ceiling you get warnings. See [Quota Warnings](quota-warnings.md).
- **[Premium feature](../getting-started/glossary.md#premium-feature)** — an extra capability a premium plan unlocks. See [Premium Features](premium-features.md).
- **[Grant](../getting-started/glossary.md#grant)** — a King sharing the kingdom's premium plan with an alliance. See [Kingdom Grants](kingdom-grants.md).
- **[Suspension](../getting-started/glossary.md#suspension)** — a paused, mostly read-only state, usually from hitting limits. See [Suspension & Limited Mode](suspension.md).

## The Subscription & Usage panel

Wherever you manage a kingdom or alliance, there's a **Subscription & Usage** panel that answers "how am I doing?" at a glance:

- Your current **plan** and where it came from (the **plan source** label).
- A **status** pill (OK, warning, critical, exceeded, or suspended).
- A **bar for each resource** showing how much of the limit you've used.
- Any **warnings** and **cleanup suggestions**.
- When the numbers were **last calculated**.

![Usage panel with warnings and exceeded limits](../images/subs-usage-warning.png)

Where you find it:

- **King** — on the kingdom's detail page (via **Kingdoms**).
- **Alliance Leader / Co-Leader** — on the **[My Alliance](../how-to/my-alliance.md)** page.
- **Supreme Admin** — everywhere, plus a platform-wide manager (covered in the admin section).

## Loading and large lists

Usage details are loaded when you open the relevant detail view rather than all at once. Large administrative lists use pagination. While a panel is loading, wait for the subject name and usage state to appear before acting; if it shows an error, refresh once and report the visible message plus kingdom/alliance scope to a Supreme Admin. See [Quota & Suspension Problems](../troubleshooting/quotas.md).

## Two ways to get premium

There are exactly two paths to unlocking premium, and both are covered step by step in this section:

1. **Request it yourself** — ask a Supreme Admin for a plan, arrange payment, and they activate it. Start at [Request a Subscription](request-subscription.md).
2. **Receive a grant from your King** — your King shares the kingdom's premium plan with your alliance, and you accept the offer. Start at [Accept a Premium Offer](accept-grant.md).

> **Important:** an alliance does **not** automatically get premium just because its kingdom is premium. Premium reaches an alliance only through a [grant](../getting-started/glossary.md#grant) the King offers and the alliance accepts, or through the alliance's own direct plan. See [Which Plan Applies to You](effective-plan.md).

| See own usage panel | ✅ (all) | ✅ (kingdom) | ✅ (alliance) | ✅ (alliance) | ✅ (own) |
| Request a subscription | ✅ | ✅ | ✅ | ✅ | — |
| Run cleanup | ✅ | ✅ | ✅ | ✅ | — |
| Offer a grant to an alliance | ✅ | ✅ (own kingdom) | — | — | — |
| Accept / decline a grant offer | ✅ | — | ✅ (own alliance) | ✅ (own alliance) | — |
| Approve requests / assign plans / suspend | ✅ only | — | — | — | — |
