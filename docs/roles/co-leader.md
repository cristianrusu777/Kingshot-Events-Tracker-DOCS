# Co-Leader Guide

This is the **start here** guide for `Co-Leader` users. The single most important thing to know is simple:

## You help run the alliance, but you do not get the delete buttons

Co-Leaders can do a lot of hands-on work, but **cannot delete** things.

That means:

- you can add and edit players
- you can enter results
- you can upload and review imports
- you can use analytics and reports
- you cannot delete players
- you cannot delete events
- you cannot delete imports
- you cannot use restore or recycle-bin actions

If you need a deletion, rollback, restore, or other risky action, that usually belongs to the `Alliance Leader`, `King`, or `Supreme Admin`.

## Premium and grants

Co-Leaders can help with alliance-side premium offers too.

Read:

- [Accept a Premium Offer](../subscriptions/accept-grant.md)
- [Premium Features](../subscriptions/premium-features.md)

Like Alliance Leaders, Co-Leaders only see the Kingdom analytics tab through an **accepted** grant that includes the kingdom analytics sharing feature.

## What only Leaders can do above you

The clearest differences are:

- deleting and restoring important records
- managing certain settings
- deeper subscription decisions for the alliance

If you are unsure whether an action is meant for you, the app usually makes that clear by hiding the button or refusing the page.
