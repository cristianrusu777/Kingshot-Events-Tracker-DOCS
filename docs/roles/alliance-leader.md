## The kingdom analytics rule

Being inside a kingdom does **not** automatically let you open the Kingdom analytics tab.

That tab appears for alliance-scoped leaders only when:

- your alliance has an **accepted** kingdom grant, and
- that grant includes the `kingdom_analytics_for_granted_alliances` feature

See [Kingdom Analytics](../analytics/kingdom.md) and [Accept a Premium Offer](../subscriptions/accept-grant.md).

## What you cannot do

Alliance Leaders cannot:

- manage users or role assignments
- create or delete alliances
- purge the recycle bin
- use the Supreme Admin subscription tools

If you hit an access refusal, check ["You don't have access"](../getting-started/access-denied.md).
