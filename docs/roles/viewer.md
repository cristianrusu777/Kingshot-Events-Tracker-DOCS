# Alliance Player Guide

This is the **start here** guide for `Alliance Player` users. It is the shortest role guide because this role is mostly read-only.

## What you can do

Alliance Players can mainly:

- read dashboards
- read players and event information
- read analytics they are allowed to see
- read rewards
- file reports

Start with:

- [Finding Your Way Around](../getting-started/navigating.md)
- [Reading the Dashboard](../getting-started/dashboard-tour.md)
- [Browse & Filter Players](../how-to/players-directory.md)
- [Read a Player Profile](../how-to/player-profile.md)
- [Analytics Overview](../analytics/overview.md)
- [Review Reward Eligibility](../how-to/rewards.md)

## What you cannot do

Alliance Players cannot:

- create or edit records
- upload imports
- enter manual results
- manage settings
- request subscriptions directly

If you need something changed, ask your `Alliance Leader` or `Co-Leader`.

## Reports: your main action path

Your most useful write action is reporting a problem or asking for help.

Read:

- [File & Track Reports](../how-to/reports.md)

If the issue is specifically about restoring something deleted, that report may lead to the restore flow described in [Request & Approve Restores](../how-to/restore-requests.md).

## When you need account changes

If your profile, role, or access needs to change, contact your alliance leadership first. They can either handle it directly or escalate it to a `King` or `Supreme Admin`.
