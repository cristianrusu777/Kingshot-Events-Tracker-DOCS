# Holding Multiple Roles

This guide is for people who hold more than one role or more than one assignment at the same time.

## The main rule: your permissions combine

The app uses a **union of permissions** rule.

That means:

- your stronger role is **not** weakened by a lesser one
- if one assignment can do something and another cannot, you still keep the stronger ability
- your visible menu is the combined result of everything you hold

For the plain-language role definitions, see [Roles Explained](overview.md).

## What this looks like in practice

Examples:

- a `King` who is also a `Co-Leader` still keeps kingdom-level access
- someone who leads more than one alliance can switch between them
- someone with kingdom and alliance responsibilities sees the wider set of pages their assignments allow

## My Alliance selector

If you lead more than one alliance, **My Alliance** shows a selector at the top so you can switch between them.

Read:

- [Use the My Alliance Page](../how-to/my-alliance.md)

## The context banner matters more for you

When you hold multiple assignments, always watch the context banner near the top of the app.

It tells you:

- which kingdom you are currently inside
- which alliance you are currently inside
- which role label is active in that context

If something looks missing, first check that banner before assuming the data is gone.

See [Finding Your Way Around](../getting-started/navigating.md).
