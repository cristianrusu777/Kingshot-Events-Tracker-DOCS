# Quota & Suspension Problems

This page is for the moments when the usage panel starts warning you, or a scope enters limited mode.

## 1. Warning banners

The usage panel has three warning levels:

- **80%** means warning: you are getting close and should plan cleanup or more capacity soon.
- **95%** means critical: you are very close and should act before normal work gets blocked.
- **100%** means exceeded: new writes for that specific resource stop until space is freed or more capacity is granted.

For the full user-side explanation, see [Quota Warnings & Exceeded Limits](../subscriptions/quota-warnings.md).

## 2. Suspension banner

If you see a suspension or limited-mode banner:

- read access still remains
- cleanup still remains available
- new write actions are blocked

Do not memorize the whole cascade model from this page. Use [Suspension & Limited Mode](../subscriptions/suspension.md) for the details, especially if a kingdom suspension may affect alliances.

Who to ask:

- `Alliance Leader` or `King` if you only need someone to review the situation
- `Supreme Admin` if the scope was manually suspended or must be unsuspended

## 3. Cleanup

If the problem is usage-based, cleanup is the fastest path out.

Use:

- [Run a Cleanup](../subscriptions/cleanup.md)
- [Use the Recycle Bin](../how-to/recycle-bin.md)
- [What "Delete" Really Does](../reference/soft-delete.md)

If cleanup is not enough, the next step is a plan change or quota increase through [Request a Subscription](../subscriptions/request-subscription.md).
