# The Online Indicator

The online indicator is a quick presence hint on the **Users** pages. It helps you see whether someone was active very recently without opening audit logs or profile history.

> Screenshot placeholder: admin-users-online.png

## What "online" means here

A user counts as online when their most recent activity was within the last 5 minutes.

That is why the indicator is only a recent-activity signal. It does not mean:

- they are looking at the same page as you
- they are ready to reply right now
- they have made a fresh change this second

## What you may see

Depending on the user's recent activity, the interface can show:

- `online now`
- `seen ... ago`
- `login ... ago`
- `no login yet`

## Where it appears

You will mainly notice it:

- in the **Users** list
- on a user's profile area inside the assignments editor
- in some admin and management lists that show responsible users

## How to use it well

- Use it as a quick clue, not as proof.
- If a user looks inactive, check their broader account details before making a decision.
- If someone never logged in, you may need to reset their password or confirm they received their account details.
