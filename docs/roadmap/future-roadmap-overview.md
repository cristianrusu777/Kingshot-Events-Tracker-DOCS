# Future Roadmap Overview

> **Planned / Future · Not implemented yet · Mixed tiers**

The roadmap explores four connected outcomes: easier daily use, more trustworthy data, better decisions, and safer ways to capture and share information.

## Free foundation

Planned **Free** work focuses on usability and trust:

- responsive mobile web layouts
- clearer navigation, setup, empty states, and dangerous-action previews
- stronger import review, duplicate detection, and confidence explanations
- basic participation and data-quality summaries
- privacy controls and fair-use guidance

## Premium Alliance direction

Planned **Premium Alliance** work focuses on running one alliance:

- alliance health scoring with an explainable breakdown
- player attendance, reliability, activity, and consistency trends
- players-at-risk and reminder suggestions
- reward-candidate and unhandled-reward suggestions
- stage, event-instance, and cross-event player analytics
- weekly alliance reports and player report cards

## Premium Kingdom direction

Planned **Premium Kingdom** work focuses on cross-alliance oversight:

- alliance comparison and kingdom event health
- growth, decline, participation, and data-coverage trends by alliance
- grant-aware analytics that respect scope and privacy
- kingdom-wide reward and event planning summaries
- monthly kingdom reports and governed Discord summaries

## Premium Both direction

Capabilities planned for both premium scopes include:

- configurable dashboards and a custom analytics builder
- premium exports to PDF, CSV, and Excel
- explainable smart recommendations
- data-quality dashboards
- scheduled summaries and notification rules

## Future Experimental direction

Research-only ideas include:

- short-video OCR with frame deduplication
- forecast ranges for participation, workload, and quota pressure
- behavior-change signals and anomaly detection
- a permission-based mobile companion app
- opt-in predictive “what if?” planning

Experimental features must be labeled as estimates, expose their evidence, and never silently change players, rewards, membership, or results.

## What this roadmap does not mean

It does not establish delivery dates, final pricing, or final tier packaging. Current capabilities remain documented outside `docs/roadmap/`.
