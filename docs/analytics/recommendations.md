# Smart Recommendations

The app uses the word **recommendations** for three different surfaces. They are related, but they are not the same thing.

For the general locked/active premium behavior, see [Premium Features](../subscriptions/premium-features.md). This page separates the three recommendation areas so you know what each one does.

>

## 1. Alliance recommendations

These live inside [Alliance Analytics](alliance.md).

When active, they can show:

- an inactivity watchlist
- follow-up suggestions for players whose participation is low or whose activity pattern looks weak

When not active, the Alliance tab shows a notice saying alliance recommendations are locked.

This is a premium surface.

## 2. Player recommendations

Player-related recommendations appear in premium analytics and event views where the app can flag people who need attention.

In practice, these are used to point out players who may need:

- follow-up
- review

This is also a premium surface. It depends on premium recommendation features and is separate from the non-premium cleanup suggestions below.

## 3. Cleanup recommendations

Cleanup suggestions appear in usage or quota panels. They are not part of the analytics tabs and they are **not** premium-only.

They help you reduce usage by pointing out safe cleanup targets, and eligible roles can run the cleanup action from there.

Typical examples are storage or history areas that have grown large enough to deserve attention.

## Why this distinction matters

If someone says "recommendations are missing," first check **which kind** they mean:

- analytics follow-up recommendations for alliances
- player-focused recommendations in premium analytics or event views
- cleanup suggestions in the usage panel

They do not unlock together in the same way.
